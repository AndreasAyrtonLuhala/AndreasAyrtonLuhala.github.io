Popcat is an Internet meme originating in 2020. The original video featuring the cat, named 'Oatmeal', chirping at a bug is posted on Twitter by its owner XavierBFB. This was later developed into an online clicker game, Popcat.click, by Joshua O'Sullivan, Edward Halis and Freddy Heppell, three Computer Science students at the University of Sheffield.
